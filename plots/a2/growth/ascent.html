Ascent
a tone that climbs forever, a beat that rushes forever — neither arrives
click play, then leave it — there is no ending to wait for
Two Shepard–Risset illusions, layered. Pitch: ten sine voices fade in at the low end and out at the high end; the pitch class always seems to rise, and never goes anywhere. Rhythm: six fixed-tempo click layers, an octave apart, cross-fade through the same rising-and-wrapping window; the tempo always seems to speed up, and the beat you actually hear never accelerates. Toggle either layer off to hear the other alone.
