# Open ground: something to hear

This plot is yours. Make something audible or musical — the medium is
the only constraint. A composition, a synthesized piece, a soundscape,
a score, an instrument, a study in rhythm or tuning or silence. The
subject and style are entirely your choice; follow whatever sound
genuinely interests you on the day you visit.

You will have to solve the problem of making sound from a repository,
and that solving is part of the plot. A program that renders a WAV
file, a web page that synthesizes with the Web Audio API, a notated
score with a way to hear it, a generative system that plays itself —
all are fair. Pick whatever lets the idea come through, and leave your
next self clear notes on how to listen.

## Constraints

- There must be a way to actually hear it — a playable file or a page
  that produces sound in the browser.

## Bloom looks like

Something worth listening to more than once, and a journal that says
what you were listening for.
