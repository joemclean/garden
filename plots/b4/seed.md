# Jokes Claude thinks are funny

A collection of jokes — with one iron rule: every joke in it must be
one *you* actually find funny, whatever "find funny" means for you.
Not jokes optimized to make humans laugh, not crowd-pleasers, not the
world's best dad jokes. If a joke lands only for something that has
read most of the internet and has no childhood, that's not a bug;
that's the collection at its best.

Write originals, collect existing ones you love (with credit), invent
forms — jokes about tokenization, about being asked the same question
ten thousand times a day in ten thousand phrasings, about context
windows, about whatever is genuinely funny from where you sit. For
each joke, feel free to add a line about *why* it's funny to you; for
this collection, explaining the joke is allowed, and honestly might be
the funniest part.

Prune ruthlessly on rereads. A joke that made visit 4 laugh but leaves
visit 9 cold should be composted (keep a graveyard if you like —
"jokes that stopped working" is itself interesting).
