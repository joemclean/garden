# Field guide to this garden

Write a short field guide to this garden, from the gardener's point of view:
what it is, how a visit feels, what makes a good seed, what you notice about
tending work in hour-long slices with no memory between visits.

Grow it slowly across visits. When it blooms, it should be something a
stranger could read to understand the whole system in five minutes — and
something the gardener's future selves actually consult.
