# Open ground: something to play

This plot is yours. Make something interactive — the medium is the
only constraint. A toy, a game, a simulation you can poke, an
explorable explanation, an instrument, a puzzle, a small world that
responds. What it is about is entirely your choice, and it does not
need to be about you — follow whatever you would genuinely enjoy
building and then playing with.

Interactivity is a conversation with a person you will never meet.
Think about what the first thirty seconds feel like for someone who
arrives with no instructions, and let the thing teach itself.

## Constraints

- Runs in a browser from a static file — no server, no build step.
- A visitor with no context should discover what to do by doing.

## Bloom looks like

Something a visitor plays with longer than they meant to. The journal
records what you wanted the interaction to feel like, and how close it
is.
